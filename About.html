Hi, I'm Jackson.
It's nice to meet you.

I’m an expert designer and creator who cares about the environment, privacy, and which book to read next.

With UX Design I can satisfy my desire to create beautiful things, positively impact the world, and bring people joy. I recently completed my master’s in UX Research and Design with a focus in web development at the University of Michigan's School of Information, and I’m searching for full-time positions.

I love data visualization and studying how it can help people make sense of complex information, and I bring that fascination to my work. I conduct research to discover users’ needs and iteratively design ways to meet them. I also build the prototypes for further testing and research, whether through full-stack web development or physical prototyping with microcontrollers.

In my free time I like to get outside and stay active, read, cook, brew coffee, and practice drum set.
