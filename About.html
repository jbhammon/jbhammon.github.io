I’m a designer who cares about the environment, privacy, and which book to read next. I believe UX design is a way I can have fun and also make a positive impact on the world. I’m currently working on my master’s in UX Research and Design at the University of Michigan and searching for internships.

Data visualization is what fascinates me, and you can often find me browsing r/dataisbeautiful when I’m procrastinating. I’m hoping to combine my technical background with the design principles I’m learning now to find new and better ways to present complicated data to users.

In my free time I like to get outside and stay active, read, cook, brew coffee, and practice drumset.
